Lewis Hamilton is a Formula 1 driver from Britain. He has driven for teams such as Mercedes-AMG Petronas and McLaren. He holds the record for the most pole positions in Formula 1 history.
Max Verstappen is a Formula 1 driver from the Netherlands. He won his first World Championship in 2021 with Red Bull Racing.
Max Verstappen was born in Hasselt, Flanders, Belgium.
Fernando Alonso is a Formula 1 driver from Spain. He has driven for teams such as Ferrari, McLaren, and Renault. He won two World Championships in 2005 and 2006 with Renault.
