DRS (Drag Reduction System) is a feature that allows drivers to open a flap on the rear wing of their car, reducing drag and increasing speed. It can only be used in designated zones during a race.
ERS (Energy Recovery System) is a hybrid technology used in Formula 1 cars to recover energy from braking and exhaust gases.
Pirelli is the sole tire supplier for Formula 1. Different tire compounds are used in races, including soft, medium, and hard, each with distinct characteristics.
